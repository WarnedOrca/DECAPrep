Explain the concept of marketing mix and how it relates to competitive advantage.

auto_awesome Performance Indicator: MT:001

The **Marketing Mix**, often referred to as the "4 Ps" (Product, Price, Place, Promotion), is the tactical toolkit used by marketers to influence consumer demand.

  • Product: The core offering that solves a customer problem. Differentiation here is key for advantage.
  • Price: Value exchange. Strategic pricing (premium vs. penetration) dictates market positioning.
  • Place: Distribution strategy. Accessibility can be a major hurdle for competitors.
  • Promotion: Communication channels. Building brand equity through consistent messaging.

"Competitive advantage is achieved when a firm crafts a Marketing Mix that is either more cost-efficient or more uniquely valuable than its rivals."
